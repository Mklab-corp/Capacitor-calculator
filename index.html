How it works

The calculator uses three fundamental capacitor formulas:

  • Charge: Q = C × V (coulombs)
  • Energy: E = ½ × C × V² (joules)
  • RC Time Constant: τ = R × C (seconds)

All values are converted to farads, volts, and ohms before calculation. The time constant τ indicates how quickly the capacitor charges or discharges through the resistor.
